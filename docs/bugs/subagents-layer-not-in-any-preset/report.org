#+title: Subagents capability is never active, so children cannot report and parents cannot dispatch
#+startup: overview

* What was reported

During a live test, a =fast-tool-user= subagent was spawned to find and
summarize a session about cybernetics.  The child completed the task, wrote its
summary to =tmp://cybernetics-summary.md=, and produced a correct one-line
result.  However, its structured =report= call did not populate =:outputs=; the
result summary came through only as the child's final assistant message.

The user asked to investigate why the =report=-with-=:outputs= path did not
work when the child called it from its own =run_elisp=.

* Observed symptom

- The child's =read=/=status= record shows a non-nil =:result-summary= (from the
  final message) but =:outputs nil=.
- The intended structured artifact list (an =org-link= to the tmp summary) never
  reached the parent's record.
