#+title: Feature Work Notes
#+startup: overview

* How To

** Feature plans
Put feature work under =docs/feats/=.

Use one directory per feature, usually with a numeric prefix and short slug:

#+begin_example
docs/feats/04-message-turn-identity/plan.org
#+end_example

Keep the plan in that directory. Add sketches or supporting notes there when they are specific to the feature.

** Research notes
Put research notes under =docs/research/=.

Use research for background investigation, comparisons, and open questions that are not yet committed to a concrete implementation plan.

When research turns into planned work, create or move the plan into =docs/feats/<feature>/= and leave the research note in =docs/research/= if it remains useful as source material.
