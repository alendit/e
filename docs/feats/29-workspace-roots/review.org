** Finding 1: secondary roots lacked discovery and sync-status regressions

*** Problem

The implementation covered secondary-root reads and the file-capability bridge,
but did not directly regress the other file surfaces named by the plan:
=glob=, =search=, and =resource_sync_status=.  Those paths thread root lists
through discovery processes and the coherence provider, so they needed explicit
coverage before marking the feature ready for review.

*** Change

=e-base-tools-test-discovers-secondary-root-resources= now verifies that a
configured secondary root works for =glob=, =search=, and
=resource_sync_status= through the public registration paths.  The same test
also asserts that paths outside all configured roots are still rejected for all
three operations.
